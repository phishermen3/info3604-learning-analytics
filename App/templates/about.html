About This Research Project

xAPI Learning Analytics Study

What Are We Researching?

This project explores the student learning process using Experience API (xAPI). xAPI allows researchers to capture meaningful learning activities such as attempts, analyses, reflections, and problem-solving behaviors across digital learning environments.

Our goal is to better understand learning patterns, engagement, and how students approach complex academic tasks.

Why Does This Matter?

Traditional grading systems only measure final outcomes. This research focuses on the learning process itself, how students learn, not just what score they receive.

Insights from this study can help improve:

  • Learning analytics and feedback systems
  • Course design and teaching strategies
  • Student support and personalized learning tools
  • Future educational technologies

What We Need From You

As a participant, you will simply use the learning system as part of your normal academic activities. Certain interactions will be recorded as anonymous xAPI learning statements.

Your participation does NOT require extra work. We are observing learning interactions, not evaluating you personally.

Your Contribution to the Research

Every interaction you make helps researchers understand how students think, experiment, and improve over time. Your participation contributes to building smarter learning systems that can benefit future students.

Data collected is used strictly for academic research purposes and will be analyzed in aggregated and anonymized form.

Privacy and Ethics

Student privacy is important to us. All collected data follows ethical research guidelines. Personal identities are not shared, and results are reported only at a group level.
