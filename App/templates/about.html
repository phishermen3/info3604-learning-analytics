About This Research Project

xAPI Learning Analytics

What Are We Researching?

This project explores the student learning process using Experience API (xAPI). xAPI allows researchers to capture meaningful learning activities such as attempts, analysis, reflection, and problem-solving behaviors across digital learning environments.

Our goal is to better understand learning patterns, engagement, and how students approach complex academic tasks.

Why Does This Matter?

Traditional grading systems focus only on final outcomes. This research instead examines the learning process itself: how students learn, not just the scores they receive.

Insights from this study can help improve:

  • • Learning analytics and feedback systems
  • • Course design and teaching strategies
  • • Student support and personalized learning tools
  • • Future educational technologies

How Do You Contribute?

As a participant, you will simply record your actions while working on your course project - from start to finish - using our logging system, LogStack. Logs are recorded as anonymous xAPI learning statements.

Logging is a quick process that you complete while working on your course project. We are observing learning interactions, not evaluating you personally. Every interaction you log helps us understand how students think, experiment, and improve over time.

Your participation contributes to building smarter learning systems that can benefit future students.

Privacy and Data Usage

All collected data is used strictly for academic research purposes and will be analyzed in aggregated and anonymized form. Personal identities are not shared, and all results are reported only at a group level in accordance with ethical research guidelines.

Student privacy is our priority.
